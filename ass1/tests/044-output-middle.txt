Signals, ensigns, and jacksof all colours were flying from her rigging, on every side. Sidewayslashed in each of her three basketed tops were two barrels of sperm;above which, in her top-mast cross-trees, you saw slender breakers ofthe same precious fluid; and nailed to her main truck was a brazenlamp.As was afterwards learned, the Bachelor had met with the mostsurprising success; all the more wonderful, for that while cruising inthe same seas numerous other vessels had gone entire months withoutsecuring a single fish. Not only had barrels of beef and bread beengiven away to make room for the far more valuable sperm, but additionalsupplemental casks had been bartered for, from the ships she had met;and these were stowed along the deck, and in the captain’s andofficers’ state-rooms.
